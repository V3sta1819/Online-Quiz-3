Give a topic and count — you can edit or delete anything it produces before saving. Optionally upload a reference file and/or list competencies to keep the AI tightly focused on your material.

Listing the exact competencies keeps every question specific to what you're teaching, instead of drifting into related-but-untaught territory.

Upload your lesson notes, module, or textbook excerpt. The AI will base questions on this content first; only if it can't reach your requested count will it add closely related questions — always staying within this material and your topic/competencies, never wandering beyond them.

No file handy? Copy-paste notes, a passage, or a lesson excerpt here instead — the AI will use it the same way: paraphrasing it into questions first, and only branching out (still within scope) if it needs more to reach your requested count.
